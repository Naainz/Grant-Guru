About Grant Guru

Empowering First-Generation Students for Financial Success

Our Mission

At Grant Guru, our mission is to empower first-generation low-income students by providing them with the tools and resources they need to achieve financial success. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue higher education without the burden of financial constraints.

What We Offer

  • Searchable and filterable database of scholarships and grants.
  • Step-by-step guides for FAFSA and CSS Profile applications and document
  • Loan comparison tool, repayment calculator, and loan forgiveness information.
  • Budget planner, expense tracker, and savings goal tracker.
  • Educational articles, video tutorials, and interactive quizzes on financial topics.
  • A simple to use Q&A Forum.
  • Student stories, peer mentoring, and discussion forums.
  • Schedule of upcoming financial aid workshops and webinars, event registration, and resource library.
